### Navigation:
`h: Move 🢀`
`j: Move 🢃`
`k: Move 🢁`
`l: Move 🡺`

`gg: Move to the 🢁 of the list`
`G: Move to the 🢃 of the list`

### Files operations:
`i: Preview`
`r: Open`

`yy: Yank (copy)`
`dd: Cut`
`pp: Paste`
`dD: Delete`

`zh: View hidden`
`cw: Rename`

### General:
`u: undo`
`z: Change settings`
